SaaS Revenue Intelligence Engine (SRIE)
Overview

The SaaS Revenue Intelligence Engine is a PostgreSQL-based analytical backend designed to unify CRM, billing, and product usage data into a single revenue intelligence system.

It enables forecasting, churn detection, expansion signals, pipeline scoring, cohort retention analysis, and executive revenue reporting.

Built and deployed on Supabase.
